What we do

Intelligence that changes how a business sees itself.

Most businesses operate without a complete picture of their own health. Financial performance is monitored. Operational reality, legal exposure, strategic clarity, and human capability are not. Problems accumulate in the gaps between what is measured and what matters.

Nicomachea develops the methodology and technology to close those gaps — producing a structured, scored, and comparable picture of a business across every dimension that actually drives performance and risk.

11
Assessment dimensions
The NIBEX framework assesses a business across eleven dimensions and sixty-eight sub-elements — from economic position and legal compliance to operational resilience and strategic clarity. Every dimension that drives business health, measured consistently.

Our flagship product

The Nicomachea Business Index.

NIBEX is a first principles business health assessment framework — the first of its kind available as a standardised, licensable methodology at the SME and institutional level.

It produces a single, comparable score from 0 to 100 across eleven weighted dimensions, with sector-specific calibration and a dereliction protocol that surfaces active legal and compliance failures before they become crises.

The before score establishes the baseline. The after score, produced on reassessment, is the proof of value — a number that demonstrates measurable improvement across every dimension addressed.
